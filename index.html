What NightMap Does

THE FULL
EXPERIENCE

01
Discover Events

Live map of every event happening near you — tonight, this week, this month. Filter by category, price and date. Never miss what's on again.

02
Buy Tickets Instantly

Buy your ticket in the app in under a minute. QR code goes straight to your phone. Show up, get scanned, you're in. No EFTs. No stress.

03
Build Your Rank

Every event you attend builds your rank. From Newcomer to Icon — your profile becomes your nightlife story. Proof that you actually live.

04
Connect with People

See who's going before you arrive. Follow people who share your vibe. NightMap is where Namibia's nightlife community lives.

05
Follow Organisers

Follow your favourite event organisers and never miss their next event. See their full history, ratings and verified status before you buy.

06
Safe & Verified

Every organiser goes through ID verification. Verified badges, strike systems and payout holds ensure every event is legitimate.
